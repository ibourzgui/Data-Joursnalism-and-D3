Healthcare Unequality in America

Correlations Between Healthcare and Poverty

The visualization of the scatter graph shows the relationship betweem poverty percent in a given state and the population percent without healthcare. States with low poverty such as Massachusetts (11.6%) has the lowest percent of people without healthcare (4.6%). The state of Mississippi has the highest poverty levels (21.5%)but the percent without healthcare (18.8%). However, the state of Texas has the highest level of population without access to heatlhcare (24.9%) but lower poverty (17.2%) in comparison to Mississippi.
